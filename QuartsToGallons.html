The formula to convert quarts to gallons is: # of quarts x 0.25 gallons

The quart (symbol: qt) is a unit of volume in the United States customary and imperial systems of measurement.

The respective versions of the quart are used mainly in the United States and the United Kingdom, though in the UK, the use of the liter is now mandated as a result of metrication.
